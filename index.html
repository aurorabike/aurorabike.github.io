Aurora Bike Studio

Welcome to Aurora Bike — a studio dedicated to building, repairing, and maintaining your dream bicycle.

I am a student at Metropolia UAS and a professional bike mechanic. In my free time, I provide bicycle repair and maintenance services for all types of bikes, including mountain bikes, road bikes, folding bikes, touring bikes, and e‑bikes. I am based in Espoo Keskus, and my service area covers the entire Helsinki Capital Region (ABC zones). You are welcome to drop off your bike at my place. If the minimum service charge reaches 40 euros, I can also offer pick‑up and delivery.

Since I work from home, please book an appointment in advance. We'll meet at Vieraskuja 5, Espoo, in front of Bar Mylly. Feel free to contact me with a photo of your bike, a description of the issue, and your location.
